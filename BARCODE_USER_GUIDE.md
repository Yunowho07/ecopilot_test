# Barcode Scanning for Disposal Guidance - User Guide

## How to Use Barcode Scanning

### Step 1: Access the Barcode Scanner
1. Open the **Disposal Guidance** screen from the main navigation
2. Look for the **"Scan or Search Product"** section
3. Tap the **barcode scanner icon** (blue card with QR code symbol)

### Step 2: Scan a Product Barcode
1. The barcode scanner camera will open
2. Position the product barcode within the **green frame**
3. Hold steady - the barcode will be detected automatically
4. Wait 3-5 seconds while the system processes

### Step 3: View Disposal Guidance
After scanning, you'll see comprehensive disposal information:

#### Product Information:
- Product name and brand
- Category (Food & Beverages, Personal Care, etc.)
- Product image
- Ingredients list
- Packaging materials

#### Environmental Analysis:
- **Eco-Score**: A-E rating (A = most eco-friendly)
- **Carbon Footprint**: Estimated CO2 emissions
- **Environmental Flags**:
  - Contains microplastics (Yes/No)
  - Palm oil derivatives (Yes/No)
  - Cruelty-free status (Yes/No)

#### Disposal Instructions:
- **Step-by-step disposal guide**: How to properly dispose of each component
- **Recycling tips**: Ways to reduce waste
- **Nearby centers**: Where to recycle or dispose
- **Visual icons**: Quick reference (recycle â™»ï¸, trash ğŸ—‘ï¸, compost ğŸŒ±)

### Step 4: Save for Future Reference
- All scanned products are automatically saved to **Recent Activity**
- Access your scan history anytime from the Disposal Guidance screen
- Review past disposal guidance whenever needed

## Troubleshooting

### "Product Not Found"
This means the barcode is not in the Open Food Facts or Open Beauty Facts databases.

**Solutions**:
1. Try scanning again to ensure barcode was read correctly
2. Use the camera feature to take a photo instead
3. Check if the barcode is valid (not promotional or internal code)

### Slow Processing
Normal processing time is 3-10 seconds:
- Barcode detection: 1-2 seconds
- Database lookup: 2-3 seconds  
- AI analysis: 3-5 seconds

If it takes longer, check your internet connection.

### Scanner Won't Open
**Possible causes**:
- Camera permission not granted
- Camera in use by another app
